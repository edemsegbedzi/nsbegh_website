About Us

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) Ghana was established in 1988 to

  • Increase the number of culturally responsible black engineers who excell academically
  • make positively impact the community
  • and promote the black community of engineers

NSBE Ghana is present in seven (7) regions in Ghana with sixteen (16) collegiate chapter and eighty-one (81) High school chapters. NSBE Ghana can boast of over three-thousand members and four (4) flagship mentoring programs.

NSBE Membership Types

Below are the NSBE membership categories with a short description

  • 01NSBE Jr. (Pre-College) Member

    Pre-college individuals who seek exposure to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM)

  • 02Collegiate Member

    Undergraduate and graduate students majoring in science, technology, engineering, and/or mathematics (STEM) at a collegiate institution or graduate level students who majored in STEM as undergraduates

  • 03NSBE Professional Member

    Working professionals and graduate students who majored in science, technology, engineering, and/or mathematics seek to fulfill NSBE's mission via avenues for networking and professional development while giving back to the community.
